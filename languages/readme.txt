LANGUAGE TRANSLATION FILES FOR CHEETAHO
---------------------------------------

If you are interested in creating a language translation for CheetahO then
you're in the right place!  We would love your help, and you
can get started translating CheetahO at https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/cheetaho-image-optimizer

Anything you can do to help is greatly appreciated and allows
CheetahO to be more easily used by people all over the world.

Thank you for supporting CheetahO!
